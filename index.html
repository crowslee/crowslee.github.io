My intent was to engage with the possibilities for playing with preexisting texts from a variety of sources (The Crying of Lot 49, No Future, Cybertext, etc.) using the way the memory of the canvas when it is uncleared to generate more text than would be convienient to store in memory to create the image. Additionally I was interested in the ways that adding interactivity to texts could be used to change their meanings, especially in relation to images via the addition or removal of context.
